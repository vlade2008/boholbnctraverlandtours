Visit the beautiful islands of Bohol

Planning for a holiday? Come and see the beautiful islands of Bohol and Cebu. Bohol is is known for the famous natural wonder of chocolate hills, the smallest monkey, the pristine beaches of Panglao & Anda, the beautiful islets for the island hopping and diving, waterfalls and also rich for its cultural heritage. Regular check-in time for those with hotel accommodations in Panglao is 2-3pm.

Likewise, Cebu also has a lot of beautiful places to visit and make your holiday even more memorable. Either place which to visit first is workable. We assist to customize your holiday visits according to your choices and schedules. Book now.
